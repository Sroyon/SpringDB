HOW TO USE THE APPLICATION :

Use http://localhost:8080/ in the following context: 
---

TO VIEW PEOPLE : 

1. Go to `/person` to view the entire record of people.
2. Go to `/person/[their id]` to pull up the record of a specific person.
3. Go to `/person/add` to add a new person into the records.
4. Go to `/person/del/[their id]` to delete a person that exists in the records.
---

TO VIEW ATTENDANCE FOR TODAY :

1. Go to `/attendance` to view the people who have been marked present today.
2. Go to `/attendance/mark/[person's id]` to mark someone present for today.
---

TO VIEW THE PEOPLE WHO ARE/WERE PRESENT ON A GIVEN DATE :

1. Go to `/[date]` to view the list of the people that were marked present on that day.
